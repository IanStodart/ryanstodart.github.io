Welcome to my tiny piece of the internet, thank you for stopping by!

This website serves to showcase some of the writing I have done over the years, both academically and recreationally. My desire in showing this is not to illustrate my work (nor my website building skills), but more specifically my writing skills and versatility, and to convey the idea that my inherent abilities and judgement are worthy of the career I am pursuing. Please take a gander at what I offer; read through the research papers and maybe learn a thing or two, ponder some of my poems, skim through one of the two short stories, whatever may pique your interest. I hope you enjoy what you see, in one form or another, and if something happens to tickle your fancy, my email and contact information are included within my resume on the last tab.

Be well, do good, live long, and prosper.
